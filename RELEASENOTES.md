Tindahan v1.0.0-beta - Initial Release

This is the first public release of Tindahan, an offline-first Android application designed specifically for local sari-sari store owners. This version focuses on essential inventory management, financial tracking, and security.

Key Features:
- Offline-First Architecture: Your data never leaves your device.
- Inventory Management: Track products with photos and low-stock alerts.
- GCash Logs: Securely record Cash In/Out with receipt images and digital signatures.
- Credit (Utang) Tracking: Manage customer debts and payment history.
- Passcode Security: Protect your store data with salted-hash encryption.
- Local Backup: Export and restore your database directly from your phone storage.

Installation:
1. Download the app-debug.apk attached below.
2. Open the file on your Android device.
3. Allow 'Install from unknown sources' if prompted.
4. Launch Tindahan and start managing your store!

Note: This is a beta release. Feedback is welcome as we work towards the stable v1.0 release.
