# Release Notes - Tindahan v1.0.0-beta

Welcome to the initial release of **Tindahan**, your offline-first companion for managing daily sari-sari store operations with ease and security.

## 🚀 Features

### 📦 Product Management
- **Inventory Tracking**: Add and manage your products locally.
- **Visual Catalog**: Attach photos to your products for quick identification.
- **Low Stock Alerts**: Set custom thresholds to know when it's time to restock.

### 💸 Financial Records (GCash)
- **Cash In / Cash Out**: Dedicated tracking for GCash transactions.
- **Digital Receipts**: Capture and store receipt images for every transaction.
- **Signatures**: Support for optional digital signatures to verify transactions.

### 📝 Utang Tracking
- **Customer Profiles**: Keep track of customers who buy on credit.
- **Payment History**: View active debts and payment history in one place.

### 🛡️ Security & Privacy
- **Offline-First**: All your data stays on your device. No internet required, no data leaves your phone.
- **Passcode Protection**: Secure your store data with a local passcode (salted and hashed for maximum security).

### 💾 Backup & Restore
- **Local Backups**: Easily export your database and images to a folder of your choice.
- **Seamless Restore**: Move your data to a new device or recover from a backup in seconds.

## 🛠️ Technical Improvements
- Built with **Jetpack Compose** for a modern, fluid user interface.
- **Room Database** ensures data integrity and fast local performance.
- **Material 3** design system for a premium look and feel.

## ⚠️ Current Limitations
- This is a **Beta** release.
- No cloud synchronization (planned for future versions).
- No shopping cart/checkout flow yet—focused on inventory and credit tracking for now.
